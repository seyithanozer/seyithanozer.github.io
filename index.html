Hello! I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the Royal College of Art School of Architecture. I am interested in the methodologies and methods of architectural knowledge and its systematisation. My current research focuses on housing design standards and regulations. I am building this website as a repository for my academic works, to learn front-end development and experiment with web-based narratives.
